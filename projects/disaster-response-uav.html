Overview

Designed, manufactured, and flight-tested a custom coaxial UAV with a 1000 mm wheelbase and a hybrid 3D-printed and carbon-fiber tube airframe. 18" rotors delivered 22 minutes of endurance at a 5 kg payload capacity, built specifically for disaster-response missions.

Autonomy & payload

  • Pixhawk flight controller paired with a Raspberry Pi companion computer
  • KML-driven autonomous missions: area scan, survivor detection, payload delivery
  • Onboard OpenCV + ML inference pipeline for real-time flood-survivor detection
  • 4G/LTE based long-range communication for beyond-visual-line-of-sight operation
  • Autonomous payload release for delivering supplies to detected survivors
