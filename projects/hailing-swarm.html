Overview

Developed a multi-drone autonomy platform combining a mothership UAV with 10 deployable child drones, designed for coordinated search, surveillance, and relay operations in GPS-denied environments.

ArduPilotROS 2DroneCANJetson Orin

Architecture

The mothership carries a Jetson Orin Nano as the primary compute platform, running the swarm coordination stack and relaying telemetry between child drones and ground control.

  • ArduPilot flight stack on all platforms, tuned per airframe
  • ROS 2 + MAVROS bridging flight controller and onboard compute
  • DroneCAN for inter-module sensor and actuator communication
  • Onboard VIO pipeline for GPS-denied position estimation

Results

Validated coordinated deployment and recovery in field tests, with stable inter-UAV communication maintained across the operational range.
