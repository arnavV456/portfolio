Overview

Built a frequency-hopping spread spectrum radio link from scratch using Ra-02 SX1278 LoRa modules and RadioLib on ESP32. The link was designed as a resilient telemetry backbone for UAV swarms operating in RF-congested or contested environments.

LoRaSX1278FHSSESP32HackRF One

Implementation

  • 16-channel LFSR-based pseudorandom hop sequence at 20 hops/sec
  • Startup sync handshake between transmitter and receiver
  • Embedded periodic resync beacons for long-term clock drift correction
  • Hop behavior validated visually on a HackRF One waterfall display

Gallery

Results

Achieved zero packet loss at bench range using SF7/BW250 settings, with an effective throughput of roughly 4800 bps after hop and sync overhead.
