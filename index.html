01 / about

I'm a final-year Electronics & Computer Engineering student at TIET (CGPA 9.34), leading avionics for Team Sammpaati — a university UAV team working on a defense-funded swarm drone logistics system under iDEX DISC14.

My work spans the full stack of autonomous systems: from PCB design and embedded firmware to multi-agent planning and RF communications. I care about systems that work in the field, not just in simulation.

Currently co-authoring an IEEE RA-L paper on communication-aware MARL for decentralized UAV swarms. Also active as a freelancer on Upwork — drone avionics, PCB design, and SITL simulation.

UAV · Embedded 2024

Dual-Band mLRS Communication Architecture

Designed a 900 MHz + 2.4 GHz redundant MAVLink telemetry link using RadioMaster Bandit and Ranger modules fed into a Jetson Orin Nano running mavlink-router. Evaluated ELRS vs mLRS for anti-jamming in iDEX defense context.

  • mLRS
  • mavlink-router
  • FHSS
  • HackRF One
  • RadioLib / ESP32
